Life is heroes of might and magic

What was so awesome about Heroes of Might and Magic III that made us so excited and wanting to play it when we were kids?

Collecting those delicious, sparkling jewels, crystals, gold and chests. Learning new magical spells. Having your own castle, upgrading buldings, recruting and upgrading army units, collecting resources and taking over the mines.

Batteling monsters and other players for experience and rewards, taking over castels, magical spells, leveling up the hero, finding and collecting artifacts, variety of army units with unique abilities and spells new territories to explore, treasures to discover, territory to conquer.

It was adventurous, magical, rich, the sound effects were beautiful, the music was a masterpiece played by actual orchestra, and the graphics were out of this world beautiful for 1999.

Life is Heroes of Might and Mgical. Were all in a super advanced game full of magical possibilities. You are a hero on a journey, think through your moves carefully, you only have so much time in a day.

Strategically position your army and and spend your responces wisely. Upgrade your talents, skills, level up and gain wisdom and experience by completing quests. Defeat monsters gurding tr easure and resource mines.

Only then you will gain true wisdom that will allow you to to learn higher level magial abilities and skills. Make sure your castle is well gurded, but at the same time, go out on adventures and explore the world, that's how you become stronger, wiser and find all the gifts and artifacts waiting for you.

And dont forget about the "holy grail", put the pieces of the puzzle together and realize what it actually is, keep digging until you find it, it's the ultimate prize!
